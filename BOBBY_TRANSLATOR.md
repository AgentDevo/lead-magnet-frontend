- **Variants:** European, Canadian, Belgian, Swiss
- **Formality:** Distinguishes tu/vous
- **Strength:** Romance language mastery
- **Special:** Handles accents, gendered nouns

### 🇳🇱 Dutch
- **Variants:** Netherlands, Flemish (Belgium)
- **Formality:** Less formal/informal distinction than French
- **Strength:** Direct, clear expression
- **Special:** Compound word handling, separable verbs

- **Formality:** Distinguishes tú/usted
- **Strength:** Wide regional usage
